About Us

Here at the Frozen Lotus Gardens, we strive to have not only the widest varieties of plants, but also plants that range on every person's skill level to take care of them. We are based here in colorful Colorado and love to use modern, rustic, and other aesthetic designs when designing out products. We want what is best for our customers and plants, so every plant will come with a care guide to ensure your new companion has what it needs to grow happily and healthy. We strive for excellent customer service and to bring new life, color, and style in every home on any budget.

About Our Plants

Our plants are imported from all over the world. We have pieces from India, China, Japan, South America, and so many more. The planters and pots that they come in are hand made and individually inspected to ensure our top quality is the only quality that you recieve. The care cheets that come with your new friends are just one of the few things that ensure they remain our top quality after it leaves our nursery. All plants will come with a two week return policy to ensure you will be happy with the results and your new companion.
